Instructions for the ultimate cheese on toast are…

Key to the fastest cheese on toast: grill it!

Turns out, there are lots of techniques for making cheese toast! Some use the oven: but who wants to wait 10 to 15 minutes for their oven to preheat?

This is an easy recipe for when you’re starving, so it needs to be fast. Right? Grill it instead!


  1. Preheat the grill. Place the bread on a baking sheet and broil it 1 to 2 minutes per side, until lightly toasted.

  2. Spread the bread with a thin coating of mayonnaise, then the Dijon mustard. Top with 1 pinch salt. Add the shredded cheese and sprinkle with oregano.

  3. Place the toast back on the baking sheet. Grill again for about 1 to 2 minutes until the cheese is melted. Enjoy!
