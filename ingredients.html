Ingredients For The Ultimate Cheese On Toast

Here's exactly what you need:

  • Sour Dough Bread.1 large slice.

  • Mayonnaise.½ tablespoon.

  • Dijon mustard.½ teaspoon.

  • Kosher salt.1 pinch.

  • Grated cheese.⅓ cup (we used a mix of mozzarella, sharp cheddar, and Parmesan).

  • Dried oregano 1 pinch.

Now continue onto the cooking instructions for how to put all of the above ingredients together and experience more cheese than your drunk uncle's jokes can provide at your second cousin's third wedding reception in the space of 7 years!
